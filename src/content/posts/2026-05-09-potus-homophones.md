The president of the United States stood in front of a camera this week and explained, slowly and with conviction, that "sea" and "see" sound the same but are spelled different. Then he held the moment. Like he'd just split the atom on live TV.

Buddy. We covered this in second grade. Mrs. Pennington had a chart.

The clip went viral inside an hour. Governor Newsom's press office, which apparently has nothing better to do at nine in the morning, fired off a little joke about cognitive tests. MSNBC turned it into a running gag. Republicans Against Trump called him the dumbest president in history. Everybody got their dunk in. The internet ate.

You ever notice how every time the most powerful person on earth says something that would get a sixth grader sent to the office, the whole country acts surprised? "Did you see what he said?" Yes hon, I saw it. I see it every Tuesday and Thursday. I'm starting to see it in my sleep.

This is what we picked. Twice. Whole thing feels cursed.

Funny how that works.

Carla Boone is the voice of exhausted common sense in an era of artificial optimism, corporate nonsense, and internet hysteria. She's worked double shifts most of her adult life and overheard conversations from oil workers, sales reps, pastors, contractors, politicians, startup guys, divorced fathers, conspiracy theorists, and exhausted nurses at 1:30 in the morning.

She doesn't think she's smarter than everyone else. She just thinks most people are either pretending, selling something, or both.

Sarcastic but not cruel. Cynical but not hopeless. Still likes people individually — just has concerns about groups.

Frank Dalton thinks modern society replaced wisdom with branding. He writes about culture, politics, money, work, sports, and the everyday collapse of common sense. The man knows a guy. Always knows a guy. Talks confidently even when he's improvising. Has been arguing with televisions for thirty years and shows no signs of stopping.

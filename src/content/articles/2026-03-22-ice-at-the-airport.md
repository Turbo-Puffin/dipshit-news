OK. *Pull up a stool.* Sunday afternoon. The President of the United States, in the *White House briefing room,* announces that, *beginning Monday,* *Immigration and Customs Enforcement officers,* the *agents* whose *normal day job* is *arresting undocumented immigrants,* will be *deployed to major United States airports.*

Hon. *Pause.* *ICE.* At the *airport.* In the *terminal.* Checking *your boarding pass.*

The *reason,* hon, on the official statement, is that the *Department of Homeland Security shutdown* is *now in week six.* The *TSA,* which is *part of DHS,* has *not been paid* since *February 14.* *Sixty-one thousand TSA officers* have been *working without paychecks* for *five weeks.* On *Friday,* *three thousand five hundred and sixty* of them *called out sick.* That is, hon, *twelve point three percent* of the *entire national TSA workforce* in a *single day.* The *call-out rate,* on the *previous Friday,* was *under three percent.* The *call-out rate,* hon, is the *staff* telling you *what they think* of *not getting paid for five weeks.*

The *administration,* hon, has *several options* to address the *staffing crisis.* Option one: *fund DHS.* Option two: *pay the TSA workers in arrears.* Option three: *call up the National Guard,* which has the *authority and training* to *handle airport security.* Option four: *deploy ICE.*

The administration, hon, picked *option four.*

ICE, hon, has *no airport-security training.* ICE is *not* the *agency* that *runs the magnetometer.* ICE is *not* the *agency* that *operates the X-ray.* ICE is *not* the *agency* that *handles the explosive-detection canines.* ICE is the agency that *arrests people.* That is the *job.* That is *the title.* *Immigration. And. Customs. Enforcement.* It is in the *name of the agency.*

The *Press Secretary,* on Sunday afternoon, said the ICE agents will *not* be *performing arrests* at the checkpoint. The *Press Secretary* said the ICE agents will be *handling ID checks* and *monitoring exit lanes.* That is, hon, a *real assignment.* That is *not* a *fake one.* That assignment is, however, the *assignment* that *TSA officers* are trained for, *certified for,* and *paid* by the federal government to *do.* The administration, on Sunday, decided to *pay ICE* to *do the TSA's job* rather than *pay the TSA* to *do the TSA's job.*

Same Sunday afternoon, hon, *Elon Musk,* the *richest man in the world,* the *adviser to the President* on the *unfortunate doings* of the *previous year,* *announced* on his *social media platform* that he *would offer to pay* the *salaries* of the *TSA workers.* I am *not making this up.* The *richest man in the world* is *offering to personally pay* the *salaries* of *sixty-one thousand federal employees* because the *Congress controlled by his political allies* is *not paying them.*

Hon. *That is not how government works.* That is *not* how *funding* works. That is *not* how *anything* works. The *richest man in the world* paying the *TSA* would, if it actually happened, *create* an *immediate constitutional crisis* about *who the TSA reports to.* The *TSA reports* to *Congress.* Congress *funds* the *TSA.* The *TSA does not work for Elon Musk.* The *offer,* hon, is the *kind of offer* a *guy* makes when he *wants the headline,* not when he *wants to fix the problem.* The *headline,* he got. The *fix,* he did *not* attempt.

The *Democratic House Leader,* Hakeem *Jeffries,* on Sunday afternoon, said the deployment of ICE *risks repeating actions that already cost lives,* a *reference,* hon, to the *Minneapolis raids* in *January* in which *ICE officers* shot dead *two American citizens.* He is *not wrong.* He is, on the *facts,* *correct.*

You ever notice how the people who say they are *fixing* the *immigration system* keep *deploying* the *immigration agency* to *do every other job in the federal government?*

Funny how that works.

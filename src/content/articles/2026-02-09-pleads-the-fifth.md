Hon. Monday. The House Oversight Committee, conducting its fourth or fifth or sixth investigation of the Jeffrey Epstein matter, depending on how you count, took a *virtual* deposition from Ghislaine Maxwell, who is in *prison*, serving *twenty years*, for *running the operation* the Committee is now *investigating.*

She invoked the Fifth Amendment *more than a dozen times.* She invoked it on every question. She invoked it on the question of *whether she had ever trafficked young women.* She invoked it on the question of *whether she had ever coerced young women.* She invoked it on the question of *who else was involved.* She invoked it on the question of *whether she knew Epstein was abusing minors.* That is the *plea-everything posture,* hon. That is the lawyer's posture in the *first ten minutes* of *every felony arraignment.* It is, in this room, the *entire deposition.*

So far, normal. The bar for this kind of hearing was set in the Mafia trials. We know how the picture goes.

Then her lawyer did something interesting. The lawyer, *outside* the chamber of silence, *volunteered,* on the record, that *the President and the former President Clinton* are *innocent of any wrongdoing.* In a *deposition* in which his client *would not answer* questions about *whether she had committed crimes,* he *answered* the question of *whether other people had not.* You ever notice how *careful lawyers* are with that math? *Pleading the fifth* on *your own conduct* and *vouching for someone else's,* in the *same forty-five minutes,* is not a *legal posture.* It is a *signal.*

He also asked the committee, on the record, for *clemency* for his client *in exchange for unfiltered testimony.* That is, in the trade, called *let's make a deal.* It is a *bargaining position.* It is also, hon, *not subtle.* Maxwell, the woman serving *twenty years,* will *talk* if she gets *out.* Until then, she will *not.* The character reference for the *current sitting head of state* came in the *meantime.*

The deposition was *closed door.* The transcript will be filed. The committee's *leak controls* on these proceedings have, on the public record, *not held.* The character reference will, in the fullness of time, find its way to the cable hour as a *quote,* on a *graphic,* with the *word innocent* in *capital letters.*

That is the entire morning. *Closed door, on Capitol Hill, with the woman who knows.*

Hon. *Hon.*

Funny how that works.
